Features

Ac Rooms

The library is equipped with air-conditioned rooms, ensuring a comfortable environment for patrons to study and read in any weather.

Personal Space

The library provides ample personal space for individuals to work and study without distractions. It may include individual study carrels or dedicated study cubicles.

Self-Checkouts

The library offers an online catalog system where users can easily search for books and other resources. Additionally, self-checkout stations allow patrons to borrow items conveniently without the need for assistance from library staff..

High Speed Wifi

Patrons can enjoy access to high-speed WIFI throughout the library premises, enabling them to conduct online research, download resources, and access digital materials seamlessly.
